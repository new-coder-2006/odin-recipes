Chocolate Cake

Description

This chocolate cake is a rich and indulgent treat, perfect for any occasion. The cake itself is wonderfully moist and fluffy, with a deep chocolate flavor that comes from using high-quality cocoa powder. Each bite melts in your mouth, providing a perfect balance of sweetness and chocolatey goodness. The cake layers are tender yet sturdy enough to hold up to the generous amount of luscious chocolate frosting that envelops them.

The chocolate frosting is a creamy, smooth delight, enhancing the cake’s flavor with its velvety texture and intense chocolate taste. It spreads effortlessly over the cake, creating a beautiful, inviting finish. This cake is not only a feast for the taste buds but also a visual delight, especially when adorned with sprinkles or chocolate shavings. Whether you’re celebrating a special occasion or simply satisfying a chocolate craving, this cake is sure to impress and delight everyone who takes a bite.

Ingredients

  • 1 3/4 cups (220g) all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 cups (300g) granulated sugar
  • 3/4 cup (75g) unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup (240ml) whole milk
  • 1/2 cup (120ml) vegetable oil
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 cup (240ml) boiling water

For the Chocolate Frosting:

  • 1 cup (230g) unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 1/2 cups (150g) unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 5 cups (625g) powdered sugar
  • 1/2 cup (120ml) whole milk
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • A pinch of salt

Steps

  1. Preheat the Oven
  2. Mix Dry Ingredients
  3. Add Wet Ingredients
  4. Add Boiling Water
  5. Bake the Cakes
  6. Cool the Cakes
